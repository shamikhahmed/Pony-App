How to Use
1
Add a Pony
Tap the + button on the collection tab to create a new pony card. Fill in the details and choose an emoji to represent your pony.
2
Organize by Generation
Use the filter buttons to organize your ponies by generation (G1-G5). Track completion for each generation on the dashboard.
3
Rarity Tracking
Each pony has a rarity level. View your rarity breakdown in the Stats tab to see collection balance.
4
Detailed Information
Add color, condition, acquisition date, pictures, and notes for each pony. Perfect for collectors tracking value and history.
5
Collection Insights
Check the stats tab to see rarity distribution, generation progress, and overall collection metrics.
6
Backup Your Data
Use the Export feature in Settings to download your collection as a JSON file for safekeeping.
